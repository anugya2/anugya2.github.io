Oasis in Arizona

The famous studio, Ranch mine surprises us with their latest design. Its a courtyard .... house in the deserts of arizona. The studio designed this to be a musician's residence. Critics have deemed it an "oasis in arizona". This is beautiful house with a courtyard system with white stucco walls and solar panels. These white walls and the courtyards were particularly insipired by the traditional courtyard houses in the deserts of Rajasthan. The white surfaces reflect sunlight and avoid trapping of heat and the courtyard provides a buffer between the harsh outside air and the controlled internal temperatures. This desgn is ideal for a desert environment. The designers have kept in mind the efficiency of power and have made the use of solar panels as well.
